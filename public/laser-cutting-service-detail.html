About Us

LASER CUTTING

In order to melt material in a specific location, a focused laser beam is used in the primary thermal process of laser cutting. The molten material is ejected and a kerf is made using a co-axial gas jet. By moving the laser beam or workpiece while using a CNC, a continuous cut is created. Fusion cutting, flame cutting, and remote cutting are the three main types of laser cutting.
